Information

This application helps you control which Overwatch servers you connect to by blocking specific regions.

How to use:

  1. Select a region you want to avoid playing in
  2. Click "Block Region" to prevent connections to that region's servers
  3. Launch Overwatch and play with better ping!

Important notes:

  • Blocking won't work if Overwatch is currently running
  • You may need to restart Overwatch after changing settings
  • All blocks will be automatically removed when you close this app
